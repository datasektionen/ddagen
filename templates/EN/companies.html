EXHIBIT AT DAGEN 2022

Why should my company participate in D-Dagen?

D-Dagen is an outstanding opportunity for Computer Science and IT companies to connect with students in different stages in their studies. Maybe you are looking for Master thesis students, part time employees, summer interns, or newly graduated MSc or PhD students? Do you want to boost your visibility as an employer, showcase your ongoing projects or share your company culture? Then don't hesitate to apply to D-Dagen!

The application process to exhibit on D-Dagen consists of three steps:

Step 1

The first step is to fill out the interest form no later than 25th of May. After the form is submitted you will receive an email informing you if you have received a spot at the fair. If you have been allocated a spot, an agreement will be attached in the email.

Registration 2022

Step 2

The next step is to sign the contract (including the order information form), scan it within three (3) weeks and send it to ansvarig@ddagen.se with the title “Contract D-Dagen 2022 - [Company name]”. Please note that if we do not receive the contract within 3 weeks from the date the contract is sent, your spot in the fair will be removed.

Step 3

During the last step you will receive a signed contract from D-Dagen within 2 weeks from the date we received the contract. The application process is thus completed.

BASE PACKAGE

  • An area of 5 m2
  • 1 table
  • Breakfast and lunch for 2 people
  • Dinner tickets to the evening event for 2 people
  • 10 bar coupons
  • Electricity and Wi-Fi
  • Access to a company lounge with fika and refreshment during the day
  • A company host who will assist you throughout the day
  • Receipt and storage of shipments

Price: 30 000 kr

Addons

It is possible to place additional orders on things that could be needed. For example, this could be extra tables or tickets to the dinner.
If you have any questions regarding this, please send an email to sales@ddagen.se

Event

There is a possibility of arranging company events in connection to D-Dagen. Please contact event@ddagen.se for more information.

Additional Information

Can we make our company visible in some other way than only participating on D-Dagen?
Please contact johan.abdi@ddagen.se and write an email containing your thoughts, and we should be able to form a solution. For example, it could consist of having the company logo on the goodiebag or something similar.
I wonder something else, who should I contact?
Send an email to ansvarig@ddagen.se, and we will make sure to answer your question.
