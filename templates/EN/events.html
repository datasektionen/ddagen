Events Associated with D-Dagen 2019


D-Dagen is not only a labor market day, but we also arrange many exciting events in association with the fair! The events are planned together with partner companies with the goal of creating more value for us students. We have events such as lunch lectures, competitions, CV events where you learn how to make your CV as attractive as possible, and informal interviews where you as a student get personal contact with companies. Further down on this page you will find the events D-Dagen organizes. Feel free to follow us on our social media for more information on registration and other updates!
