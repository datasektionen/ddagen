What is D-dagen?
D-Dagen is the KTH Computer Science chapterâ€™s yearly job fair. The event is aimed toward providing companies and students within the Computer Science and IT sector an opportunity to meet and get to know each other. The project group behind D-Dagen wants to help establish a more direct and personal contact between company representatives and students.

How many visitors usually attend D-Dagen?
2019 D-Dagen had 2000 visitors and 2020 D-Dagen had about a 1000 digital attendees.
Who is D-dagen for?
D-Dagen is for students active within the Computer Science and IT sector.
What is the D-Dagen catalogue and where can I find the catalogues from previous years?
The D-Dagen catalogue is given out to visitors in connection with the event every year. It is a collection of the most important information about the fair as well as information about the participating companies. The catalogue contains a map over the placement of the company booths. This year the catalogue will be available in an electronic format at ddagen.se and only a condensed brochure will be printed in order to minimize our climate impact. The electronic catalogue will also allow for search- and filter functions and will increase the usability.
